Office tiles + character sheets in this folder were generated by scripts/gen_assets.py
(16x16 office palette, itch.io-style free office vibe) for the Phase 1 MVP.

Audio (also procedural, CC0-equivalent / self-generated — no third-party samples):
- office-ambient.wav — soft loopable ambient pad
- sfx-running.wav / sfx-blocked.wav — short status one-shots

To swap in a real itch.io CC0/free office tileset later:
1. Drop the PNG as office-tiles.png (keep 16x16 tile size or update map JSON).
2. Remap GIDs in office-map.json / Tiled.
3. Keep collision layer semantics (non-zero = blocked).
